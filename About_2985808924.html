​Our mission

​​Introducing tourists to tourist destinations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

​There are many tourist destinations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, such as historic Jeddah and King Fahd's Fountain in Jeddah, historic Diriyah, Al Faisaliah Tower, and Kingdom Tower in Riyadh, and many other tourist destinations.

​Introducing tourists to Saudi culture

​Many foreign tourists do not know about Saudi culture, and here comes our mission to introduce them to our culture, for example, introducing them to traditional Saudi clothing, and also introducing them to Saudi folk dances and food.
